# Febri Kurniawan

Traffic safety researcher with a background in transportation engineering.
My research focuses on crash data analytics, injury severity and vehicle damage modeling,
and the application of machine learning and statistical methods for traffic safety analysis.

## Research Interests
- Traffic safety and crash analysis
- Injury severity and vehicle damage modeling
- Machine learning and statistical modeling in transportation
- Big data analytics for crash risk assessment
- Disparity and fairness analysis in crash outcomes

## Methods & Tools
- Statistical Modeling: Logistic Regression, Generalized Estimating Equations (GEE), Structural Equation Modeling (SEM)
- Machine Learning: Random Forest, Gradient Boosting, Bayesian Networks
- Data Processing: Feature selection, multicollinearity assessment, imbalance handling, fairness-aware resampling
- Software & Tools: Python, R, SPSS, Microsoft Excel
